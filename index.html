Meet Your Dental Team

Welcome to one of Alaska's most advanced dental practices–Right here in Anchorage!

Aurora Family Dentistry, PC would like to welcome you to a fantastic dental experience!

 Our team will make you and your family more comfortable than you ever thought possible!

Dr. Lyke is a member of the American Dental Association, the Alaska Dental Association, and the Anchorage Dental Society.

Scott Lyke, DMD

Dr. Scott Lyke holds a doctorate from the prestigious Oregon Health and Science University School of Dentistry where he graduated with honors - even graduating at the top of his class in clinical dentistry. He has practiced general and cosmetic dentistry in both Oregon and Anchorage for a number of years, and he has earned extensive postdoctoral studies in cosmetic dentistry, treatment planning, and implant dentistry.

Dr. Lyke's excellent clinical skills are exceeded only by his compassion and care for patients. He takes the time to listen and get to know each patient one-on-one - helping them make informed choices about their dental health.

Dr. Jaimie Lyke is a member of the American Dental Association, the Alaska Dental Association, and the Anchorage Dental Society.

Jaimie Lyke, DMD

Dr. Jaimie Lyke graduated with honors from Oregon State in 2005 with a Bachelor of Science degree. She holds a doctorate from the prestigious Oregon Health and Science University School of Dentistry, where she graduated in 2009.

Dr. Lyke's compassionate chairside manner is enhanced by the fact that she genuinely enjoys meeting new people.

Outside of the office, Dr. Lyke enjoys playing volleyball, cross country skiing, hiking, and spending time with her daughter Chelsea.

Dr. Biederman is a member of the American Dental Association, the Alaska Dental Association, and the Anchorage Dental Society.

Paul Biederman, DDS

Dr. Paul Biederman was born and raised in Alaska and earned his doctorate from the University of Minnesota. Each year he continues to study advanced cosmetic and family dental care. Because of this, you can be assured that all of your treatment, from simple to complex, is performed with a meticulous attention to detail. It's a perfect combination - excellent dentistry and one-on-one care.

Whether you are looking for the latest in cosmetic dentistry, or just looking for a dentist who can help your family smile, please join us. We'll give you something to smile about!
